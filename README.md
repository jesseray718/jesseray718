## The Thermal Loop

Fresh outside air → Desiccant (strips humidity at intake) → Underground labyrinth (tunnel FILLED SOLID with wet open-cell aerocement, air forced through millions of pores, 35°F output) → Cold Tank B (copper coil, radiative lid) → Panel inlet → Solar panel (air through volumetric aerocement, 98% absorption, 77°C output) → Hot Tank A (copper coil, heat-sink lid) → Stirling engine (between tanks, belt drive) → back to desiccant.

**Verified per m²:** 931W in → 2,197W useful out. COP 8,544. 0W grid.
