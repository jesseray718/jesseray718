## The Thermal Loop

At the heart of OpenRoot lies the thermal loop — a **passive** solar-thermal system (zero ongoing energy input).

**Precise flow (non-negotiable order):**

Desiccant (humidity strip at fresh air intake) → Underground labyrinth (**FILLED SOLID** with wet open-cell aerocement, **NOT** lined walls, 500–2000 m²/m³ surface area, 35°F output at 100°F ambient) → Cold Tank B (copper coil, radiative lid) → Solar panel inlet → Solar panel (aerocement volumetric fill, 98% solar absorption, 77°C output) → Hot Tank A (copper coil, heat-sink lid) → Stirling engine (between Tank A and B, belt drive) → back to desiccant.

**Parasitic flow note**: As heat flows from point of capture to point of storage, it passively forces an equal volume of air through the tunnel, capturing the energy equivalent to reducing air from ambient to 35°F. The system delivers a **219.7% functional service multiplier** via ambient differentials and passive transport — **never** claimed as thermodynamic efficiency above 100%.

## Community Guidelines

1. **Open-Source**: All code, designs, and documentation will be made available under an open-source license.
2. **Collaboration**: We encourage constructive debate and discussion to drive innovation and improvement.
3. **No Patents**: OpenRoot will never seek or hold patents, ensuring that our work remains accessible to all.
4. **Greatest Good, Least Effort**: We strive to minimize waste and maximize efficiency in all aspects of our work.

## Contribution Rewards

We believe in rewarding contributors for their hard work and innovative ideas. Our reward system is based on **proof of physical work**, adoption of mesh networks, and implementation of the panel/energy system. Examples of rewards include:

- First functional blueprint and code for a functional 3D printable vehicle: 10,000 ACRE
- Successful implementation of a mesh network in a rural community: 5,000 ACRE
- Development of a new, more efficient solar panel design: 20,000 ACRE

See `/bounties` for current active bounties and `/tokens` for ACRE minting rules (minted only for verified physical work with community sign-off).
