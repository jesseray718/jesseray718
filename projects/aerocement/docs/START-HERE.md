## Make Your Own Activated Carbon
1. Fill metal can with dry wood chunks
2. Punch one small hole in lid
3. Heat in fire 45-60 minutes until smoke stops
4. Let cool completely before opening
5. Crush black carbon into powder

## Mix Recipe (Xanthan Gum Method)
1. Mix xanthan gum into rubbing alcohol until smooth
2. Add 1 liter water + Dawn Ultra, stir gently
3. Blend activated carbon into dry cement (5-6% by weight)
4. Add cement to liquid, stir 3-5 minutes
5. Keep stirring past the collapse point — this creates
   the open-cell structure. Air flows through, not around.

## The 21-Day Cure
Cover with plastic. Spray with water twice daily.
Keep visibly damp for 21 full days. No exceptions.
Dry early = brittle structure that fails in 5 years.
Proper cure = structure that lasts 50+ years.

## Sensor Points

2. Panel outlet (hot side)
3. Desiccant outlet
4. Underground tunnel outlet (cold side)
