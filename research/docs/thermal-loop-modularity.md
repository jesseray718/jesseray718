# Thermal Loop Modularity — Panels Stand Alone or Scale

Each volumetric aerocement solar panel is modular with inlet + outlet.

**Standalone (easiest community starting point):** Fresh air in → volumetric aerocement matrix (98 % capture) → hot air out for direct use. Passive draft sufficient. No loop, no tanks, no engine required. Poorest communities can build one panel first for immediate utility.

**Full loop:** Adds the solid-filled labyrinth cold sink and tanks; parasitic flow drives air without fans.
